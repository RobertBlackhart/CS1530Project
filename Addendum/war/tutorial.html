Addendum is a simple social network aimed at helping students with their classes.
Here's how to make the most of it.

Profile


Your profile is how your classmates and other addendum users can learn a little bit more about you.
You can access your profile by clicking any link that contains your username.

Change Password allows you to change your password by putting in a new one and confirming it.

The Trophy Case shows you what trophies you've earned, and which you have yet to achieve.
Trophies are earned by regularly interacting with Addendum! They have varying degrees of difficulty to get.

Clicking the Posts tab lists all the posts you've made in reverse chronological order.


You can also provide addition information about yourself, like your college, real name, and contact info.
This helps other users in your class identify you, get in contact with you outside of Addendum and learn a little bit about you.

You can also look at the profile of any other Addendum users to see this information about them.

Class Feed

Here's what your class feed will look like. We'll go through a few key functionalities of Addendum.


Right above the posts are tabs for New and Popular. Clicking on a certain tab will yield the same posts, but sorted either by date posted or popularity, respectively.

Search for a Post will do just that: if you put a search term in the field, it will return a tab of all posts that contain that search term.

Above your username is a default icon. Hover over that image to produce a Change Image button. This allows you to upload a picture that will accompany all your posts.
Square icons are recommended.


Below your icon and username is the link All Classes and Add a New Class. Adding a class is as simple as filling in one search term.
Looking for a Computer Science class? Boom, look up 'CS'.
Once a class is added, you can look at posts specifically for that class or posts for everyone.
Both posts for everyone and for your classes specifically will show up in your All Classes link.


Create a New Post will give you a prompt box to type your post in. You can use the provided tools to edit your post or you can use HTML.
You can also embed YouTube videos and attach files you think your classmates may find important.

Excellent posts can be upvoted, which increase their popularity. You can also downvote less-than stellar posts, and comment on posts. Brilliant responses to posts can be checked by the user who originally wrote the post.

That's the gist of Addendum! Enjoy the site and Happy Studying!
